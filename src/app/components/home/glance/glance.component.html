STRONGER TOGETHER AT A GLANCE

A Distinct Identity

Stronger Together was founded in October 2019 and continues to grow and build with the support of the community. Here at Stronger Together, we believe in creating a community in which no-one is left behind. We continuously try to raise awareness, provide motivation and support, and break down stigma. Take a look around our website to see the various services we offer, along with our blog posts, and the forum page to allow you to journal, connect, and discuss with one another. Our shop will be coming soon, and its primary goals will be to provide essentials that will help our well-being, and boost self-care - a portion of the proceeds will be donated to Centre for Addiction and Mental Health (CAMH).
